Jonny Swan, here. This site is just a bunch of stuff that I've done... I started documenting the stuff I do because I got sick of reinventing the wheel.

I like trying new stuff and challenging myself. Once I've done it once, I don't really care much for doing it again. Unfortunately, after a few years if I do have to do something over again, I have no idea how I did it the first time. And that, my internet friends, makes me very unhappy.

This site will help the future me remember how/why I did something and hopefully help some of you people out on the interwebs get something done you're trying to do.


Email me at jonny@thismighthelp.info if you have any tips on my projects or ideas for new things to try.
